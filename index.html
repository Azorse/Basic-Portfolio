Dan Schissler

About Me

Dan is a Consultant with Safety In Motion, Inc. and has been on staff since March 2015.
His duties include presenting Safety In Motion® training seminars to client leaders and
employees, developing internal subject matter experts, and guiding employees in their
understanding and application of Safety in Motion® techniques and principles.

Dan has a Bachelors of Science degree in Philosophy and prior to employment at Safety in Motion, Inc.
spent ten years working in hospitality. There he spent many years working with internal safety teams
with a focus on risk assessment teaching better practices and on the job techniques.

In his free time he enjoys reading and outdoor activities, including white water rafting, snowboarding, hiking and golf.
